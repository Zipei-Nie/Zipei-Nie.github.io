Final Exam Details

Here are some details about the final exam. Please check your assigned location carefully, as the rooms and section assignments are different from the midterms.

The final exam will take place on Tuesday, May 12, from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM. Please arrive 15 minutes early to be seated.

  • 100 Noyes Laboratory: Siqi Jiao’s discussion sections (BDC, BDD, BDF, BDM) and Nikolay Sheshko’s discussion section (BM1).

Conflict Exam

Under university policy, a final exam conflict includes having two synchronous final exams scheduled at overlapping times, or having three or more final exams with starting times all within a 24-hour window. If you qualify for a conflict exam, please email me as soon as possible.

The conflict exam will take place in 100 Materials Science & Engineering Building on Thursday, May 14, from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM. Please arrive 15 minutes early to be seated.
